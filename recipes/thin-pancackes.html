Thin Pancakes

Description

Learn how to make the perfect thin pancakes.

Easy to do tips and tricks to get delicious and lacy pancakes.

Quick and simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • Milk - 500ml
  • eggs - 3pcs
  • flour - 200gr
  • oil - 2 table spoons
  • sugar - 2 table spoons
  • salt - 1/2 tea spoon

Steps

  1. Mix eggs, sugar and salt in a bowl
  2. Add milk to the bowl and mix
  3. Add flour to the bowl and mix
  4. Add oil to the bowl and mix
  5. Heat the pan on the medium fire
  6. Start making pancakes using as less dough as possiple

    7. Note: add some oil on the pan before making every pancake
