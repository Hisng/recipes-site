Salad

Description

Learn how to make the perfect salad.

Easy to do tips and tricks to get delicious salad.

Quick and simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • Tomatos
  • Cucumber
  • Onions
  • Avocado
  • Letuce
  • Salt and oil

Steps

  1. Slice cucumber, tomatos, onions, Letuce and avocado
  2. Put everything in one big bowl
  3. Add some salt and oil
  4. Carefuly mix everything
