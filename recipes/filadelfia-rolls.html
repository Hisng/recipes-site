Thin Pancakes

Description

Learn how to make the perfect filadelfia rolls.

Easy to do tips and tricks to get delicious sushi.

Quick and simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Salted Salmon
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Filadelfia cheese
  • Nori

Steps

  1. Slice cucumber and avocado to thin long pieces
  2. Prepare sushi rice
  3. Slice salmon to thin slices
  4. Put a half of nori and cover it with rice

    5. Note: you can use water to keep your hands wet while you work with rice

  5. Flip the nori
  6. Put cucumber and avocado slices, add some cheese
  7. Roll it into sushi roll and put salmon slices on top
  8. Slice the roll into 6-8 even pieces
